A U T I S M

The project "Sochana" is aimed at identifying dyslexia, dyscalculia, and autism in students. It uses various assessment techniques and tools to evaluate

  • cognitive abilities
  • language skills
  • academic performance
. The project aims to raise awareness about these conditions and provide support and intervention for students with these challenges.

This survey is specially designed for students to test if they are autistic or not. Autism is a lifelong, nonprogressive neurological disorder typically appearing before the age of three years. The word “autism” means a developmental disability significantly affecting verbal and non-verbal communication and social interaction.
